About the Project

We are Team 4, a group of four students from Ramaiah Institute of Technology pursuing B.E. in Information Science and Engineering. Our project is developed for Dezine Thinking, aimed at creating a crowd-sourced disaster management web application.

Our goal is to provide an intuitive and user-friendly platform that enables individuals and communities to respond to and manage disasters effectively. The app offers various features such as incident reporting, location sharing, survival guides, and real-time updates to empower users during emergencies.
